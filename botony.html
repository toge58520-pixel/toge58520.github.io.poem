Botany - Syllabus (B.Sc)

Botany is the science of plants. It studies plant structure, growth, reproduction, and classification. Plants are vital for life because they produce oxygen and food. Botany helps in agriculture, medicine, environmental conservation, and biodiversity studies.

📘 B.Sc 1st Year - Botany

  • Plant Diversity and Classification
  • Cell Structure and Function
  • Plant Anatomy
  • Photosynthesis and Respiration
  • Genetics and Evolution

📘 B.Sc 2nd Year - Botany

  • Plant Physiology
  • Plant Ecology
  • Economic Botany
  • Plant Biotechnology
  • Environmental Botany

📘 B.Sc 3rd Year - Botany

  • Plant Pathology
  • Genetic Engineering
  • Microbiology
  • Advanced Plant Ecology
  • Research Methods in Botany
