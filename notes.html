🌿 Botany Notes

1st Year - Plants & Classification

Botany studies plants, their structure and classification.

Mango (Mangifera indica)

Fruit tree – provides food and oxygen.

Neem (Azadirachta indica)

Medicinal plant – used for skin diseases and immunity.

Bamboo (Bambusoideae)

Fast growing plant – used in construction and furniture.

Cactus (Cactaceae)

Desert plant – stores water.

2nd Year - Plant Physiology

Photosynthesis, respiration, transpiration and plant hormones (Auxin, Gibberellins).

3rd Year - Biotechnology & Pathology

Genetic engineering, plant diseases and environmental botany.

🐾 Zoology Notes

1st Year - Animal Classification

Animals are classified as mammals, reptiles, birds, amphibians and insects.

Lion (Panthera leo)

Mammal – carnivore and top predator.

Elephant (Elephas maximus)

Mammal – largest land animal, herbivore.

Butterfly (Insect)

Insect – helps in pollination.

Honey Bee (Insect)

Produces honey and pollinates flowers.

2nd Year - Physiology

Digestive system, respiratory system, circulatory system and reproduction.

3rd Year - Genetics & Immunology

DNA structure, immune system and biotechnology in animals.

🧪 Chemistry Notes

1st Year - Basic Chemistry

Matter, atoms, molecules and chemical reactions.

Water (H2O)

Essential for life and chemical reactions.

Oxygen (O2)

Required for respiration and combustion.

Carbon Dioxide (CO2)

Plants use CO2 for photosynthesis.

Sodium Chloride (NaCl)

Common salt used in food.

2nd Year - Industrial Chemistry

Sulfuric acid, ammonia, polymers and organic chemistry.

3rd Year - Environmental & Analytical Chemistry

Pollution control, green chemistry and chemical analysis.
