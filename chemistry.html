Chemistry - Detailed Study

Chemistry is the scientific study of matter and chemical reactions. Everything around us is made of matter. Major chemical examples: Water (H2O) – essential for life Oxygen (O2) – respiration Carbon Dioxide (CO2) – plants use in photosynthesis Sodium Chloride (NaCl) – common salt Glucose (C6H12O6) – energy source Sulfuric Acid (H2SO4) – industrial use Ammonia (NH3) – fertilizers Methane (CH4) – natural gas Ethanol (C2H5OH) – medicines Calcium Carbonate (CaCO3) – construction Branches: - Physical Chemistry - Organic Chemistry - Inorganic Chemistry Uses: Medicine, agriculture, industry, environmental science
