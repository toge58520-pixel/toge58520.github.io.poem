Botany Summary

Botany is the study of plants. It includes plant structure, growth, reproduction and classification. Plants produce food through photosynthesis.

Notes

• Plants make food using sunlight. • Roots absorb water. • Leaves perform photosynthesis.

Zoology Summary

Zoology is the study of animals. It includes animal structure, behavior, evolution and classification.

Notes

• Animals depend on plants for food. • Vertebrates have backbone. • Invertebrates do not have backbone.

Chemistry Summary

Chemistry is the study of matter, elements and chemical reactions. It explains how substances interact and change.

Notes

• Matter has mass and volume. • Atoms combine to form molecules. • Chemical reactions change substances.
