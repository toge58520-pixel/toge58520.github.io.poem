Welcome Students

This website helps BSc Biology students learn syllabus topics, read summaries and test knowledge with quiz questions.

BSc Biology 1st Year

Botany

Topics include plant cell biology, algae study and plant diversity. Students learn how plants grow and perform photosynthesis.

Zoology

Covers animal diversity, non-chordates and cell biology. Students study structure and functions of animals.

Chemistry

Topics include atomic structure, chemical bonding and basic organic chemistry reactions.

BSc Biology 2nd Year

Second year focuses on plant classification, chordates, genetics basics and organic chemistry reactions. Students learn evolution, plant systematics and physiology.

BSc Biology 3rd Year

Final year includes biotechnology, ecology, evolution, wildlife conservation and advanced chemistry topics. Students study applications of biology in research.
