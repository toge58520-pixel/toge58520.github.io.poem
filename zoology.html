Zoology - Detailed Study

Zoology is the branch of biology that studies animals, their structure, classification, and evolution. Animals are essential for ecological balance and biodiversity. Major animal examples: Lion – mammal, carnivore Elephant – mammal, herbivore Tiger – carnivore Cow – domestic mammal Dog – loyal domestic animal Butterfly – insect, helps in pollination Honey Bee – insect, produces honey Ant – social insect Snake – reptile Frog – amphibian Classification: - Kingdom: Animalia - Groups: Mammals, Reptiles, Birds, Amphibians, Insects - Importance: Ecosystem balance, medical research
